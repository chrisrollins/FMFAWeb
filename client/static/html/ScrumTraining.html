SCRUM 101


This is an intense 2 day class designed to give the student all of the knowledge and information required to pass the Professional Scrum Master I certification exam administered by Scrum.org. Learn More

Comprehensive Scrum training for people wishing to earn the "Professional Scrum Master level I (PSM I) or the "Professional Scrum Product Owner" (PSPO) certifications from The creators of Scrum at Scrum.org

The Scrum Framework is revolutionizing the software development industry, providing productivity and quality improvements of 70% or more over traditional project management techniques.

According to the creators of Scrum, Scrum is:

  • Lightweight
  • Simple to understand
  • Difficult to master

In other words, adopting the Scrum framework promises breathtaking benefits, but ONLY if implemented properly

Scrum 101 is an intensive 2 day training seminar designed to give developers and project and product managers the knowledge and information necessary to pass the rigorous certification examinations administered by Scrum.org.

Scrum 101 is taught by internationally recognized Scrum Framework expert Frederik M. Fowler. Mr. Fowler is one of only about 50 individuals in the United States who holds the prestigious “Professional Scrum Expert” (PSE) certification. Mr. Fowler provides training and consulting services to help organizations adopt the Scrum Framework effectively and successfully.

Case based preparation for taking the advanced level Professional Scrum Master II (PSM II) assessment administered by Scrum.org.

There are approximately 500,000 Certified Scrum Masters (CSM) certification holders worldwide. There are also about, 40,000 Professional Scrum Master I (PSM I) certificate holders worldwide. These certifications indicate that the recipient has demonstrated “basic level” knowledge of the Scrum Framework and is competent to serve as a Scrum Master on a Scrum team.

There is also an advanced certification called “Professional Scrum Master II (PSM II). This certification indicates that the recipient has demonstrated “advanced/expert” level knowledge of Scrum. Earning this certification is quite difficult. There are only about 250 PSM II holders worldwide.
