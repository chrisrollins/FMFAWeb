Scrum Bootcamp


This is an intense 2 day class designed to give the student all of the knowledge and information required to pass the Professional Scrum Master I certification exam administered by Scrum.org.

Cost: $1,500 (includes 1 FREE attempt at the PSM I exam)Schedule Now



Putting Scrum & Jira into Practice

A 6 week college course in which students will learn the Scrum Framework, the Jira project tracking system, and the practical application of both. Students will form Scrum teams and work on real software development projects, producing software tools for use by local not for profit agencies.

After taking the class, students will have learned all of the knowledge and information required to pass the Professional Scrum Master I certification exam administered by Scrum.org.

In addition, after successful completion of the class project the student will have some solid practical experience that can be included on a resume.

Cost: $3,000 (includes 1 FREE attempt at the PSM I exam)Schedule Now
